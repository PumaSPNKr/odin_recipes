Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked dish made by layering flat pasta sheets with rich tomato sauce, creamy ricotta or béchamel, savory ground meat, and melted cheese.
Once assembled, it's baked until bubbly and golden, then rested briefly before serving.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (oven-ready or boiled)
  • Ground beef (or Italian sausage)
  • Ricotta cheese (mixed with egg & parsley)
  • Tomato sauce (marinara or meat-based)
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded, for melting)

Directions

  1. Prep Ingredients: Cook ground meat, sauté onions/garlic, mix ricotta with egg/parsley.
  2. Layer Sauce: Spread tomato sauce on pan bottom.
  3. Assemble Layers: Alternate pasta, meat, ricotta mix, sauce, repeat.
  4. Top & Bake: Finish with pasta, sauce, mozzarella/parmesan; cover, bake 25 mins, uncover for 10.
  5. Rest & Serve: Let sit 10 minutes, then cut.
