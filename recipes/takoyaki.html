Pho

Description

Pho is a fragrant Vietnamese noodle soup featuring aromatic broth (typically beef or chicken), rice noodles, fresh herbs (like Thai basil and cilantro),
and tender meat (often sliced beef or chicken). Its broth is simmered for hours with spices like star anise, cinnamon, and ginger, then served with lime, bean sprouts, and chili for customization.

Ingredients

  • Beef or chicken broth (simmered with spices like star anise, cinnamon, cloves, and ginger).
  • Rice noodles (flat, thin bánh phở).
  • Thinly sliced raw or cooked beef (e.g., flank, brisket) or chicken.
  • Fresh herbs (Thai basil, cilantro, green onions).
  • Fish sauce (for seasoning the broth).

Directions

  1. Simmer broth: Boil bones (beef/chicken) with charred onion/ginger, star anise, cinnamon, cloves.
  2. Prep noodles: Cook rice noodles, drain.
  3. Assemble: Layer noodles, raw/cooked meat in bowls.
  4. Ladle broth: Pour hot broth over meat to cook it.
  5. Top: Add herbs, bean sprouts, lime, chili. Serve.
